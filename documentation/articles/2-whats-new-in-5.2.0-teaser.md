**🚀 Kotools Types 5.2.0: a faster `Integer`, exact `Decimal` arithmetic, and
sign as a type**

This release reworks `Integer`'s internal representation for speed, adds a
brand-new `Decimal` type for exact arithmetic (no more `0.1 + 0.2 != 0.3`),
makes `Integer` division Euclidean and total, and introduces `NonZeroInteger`
/ `NonNegativeInteger` / `NonPositiveInteger` to turn sign constraints into
types.

We wrote a short tour of all four changes, each linking to its own deep-dive
article.
